A total 25.628 Sqm neighborhood square in a densely populated and ill-urbanized neighborhood of Gaziosmanpaşa district locates in the north of E5 and south of TEM international highways in the European side of Istanbul. The square has significant problems in terms of fragmented pedestrian movement and interrupting vehicular traffic, poor accessibility, lack of environmental maintenance, dereliction and lost place identity. The urban design project that has been developed for this square draws a subtle scenario of inviting everyone from every age and social background to the square to enjoy the landscape while living daily lives in a friendly, healthy, safe and accessible square.
