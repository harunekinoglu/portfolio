Urban Design in the Creative Intersection of Space, Culture and Identity | Presentation

As an invited speaker, I conducted a presentation on topic of urban design in the creative intersection of space, culture and identity for Istanbul Design Week & Human Cities Festival: Istanbul-Brussels F,estconducted a presentatione: “Re-making of Public Space” (Istanbul, Turkey)

Human Cities Brussels Istanbul was held during Istanbul Design Week at the Old Galata Bridge. This international 2-day event hosted lectures, breakout sessions and workshops for architects, urban planners, sociologists, designers, artists and representatives of the civil society (associations and NGO’s involved in urban culture, creativity and intercultural dialogue) from Turkey and various European countries. In collaboration with IDW, further activities included an international exhibition, public performances, urban walks and itineraries, etc. “Human Cities Brussels-Istanbul Festival 2010” underlined the importance of the relationship between contemporary creation (arts/design), quality of the public spaces, and sustainable development in Istanbul. As a young urban design professional and invited speaker from the Istanbul Metropolitan Municipality, Department of Urban Design, I gave a lecture on urban Design in the creative intersection of space, culture and identity.
